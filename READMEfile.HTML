Delete Your Account - XTraveler

If you wish to delete your XTraveler account and all data associated with it, you can request deletion at any time by following the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the XTraveler app on your device.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion request. Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently removed.

Alternatively, you can send us an email at xtravelerr@gmail.com using the same email or phone number linked to your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Data Deletion Details

  • All personal data (name, profile photo, phone number, and trip details) will be permanently deleted from our servers.
  • Some anonymous usage data and system logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security and fraud-prevention purposes, after which they are automatically erased.
  • Once your account is deleted, it cannot be restored.

This page is maintained by the XTraveler development team to comply with Google Play’s account deletion requirements.

For any questions or support regarding your data, please contact us at xtravelerr@gmail.com.
